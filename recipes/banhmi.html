Banh Mi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Baguette
  • Pork meat
  • Pickled carrot
  • Cucumber
  • Mayonnaise
  • Vietnamese herbs

Instructions:

  1. Toast the baguette and slice it in half.
  2. Cook the pork meat and slice it into thin strips.
  3. Assemble the sandwich with the sliced pork, pickled carrot, cucumber, and mayonnaise.
  4. Garnish with Vietnamese herbs and serve hot.
